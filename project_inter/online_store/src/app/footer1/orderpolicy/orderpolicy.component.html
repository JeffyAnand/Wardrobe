Order Policies

1. Shipping and Delivery Policies

  • Clearly state shipping options (standard, expedited, etc.) and estimated delivery times.
  • Specify any shipping fees or minimum order requirements.
  • Address international shipping and customs duties if applicable.

2. Return and Exchange Policies

  • Outline the process for returns and exchanges.
  • Include conditions (e.g., unworn, tags attached) and timeframes (e.g., within 30 days).
  • Explain who covers return shipping costs.

3. Cancellation Policies

  • Describe how customers can cancel orders (before and after shipment).
  • Address any restocking fees or cancellation deadlines.

4. Payment Policies

  • Detail accepted payment methods (credit cards, PayPal, etc.).
  • Highlight secure payment processing.
  • Mention any pre-authorization holds for pending orders.

5. Privacy and Data Protection

Refer to your Privacy Policy (as discussed earlier) regarding data collection, storage, and security. Assure customers that their information is protected.

6. Customer Support and Communication

  • Provide contact information for customer inquiries.
  • Set expectations for response times (e.g., within 24 hours).

7. Size Charts and Fit Guidelines

Include detailed size charts and fitting advice to help customers make informed choices and reduce returns.
